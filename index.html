    About

    Kanji Sensei is a smartphone app that helps you learn kanji and vocabulary easily. No need to search and download decks online. Kanji sensei offers kanji writing practice with stroke recognition, kanji reading in context (within words), meaning, and kanji extended info out of the box.

    The app is currently in pre-beta and under heavy development. Please help testing it while learning japanese!

    ℹ️ Kanji Sensei is currently available for iOS devices only. Moreover, it's a pre-beta version of the app and thus some features in the roadmap are still missing. If you'd like to contribute to its development, please drop me a line.

    FAQ

    Why Kanji Sensei over Anki or Quizzlet?

    If you're anything like me, you want your kanji studied anywhere (train commute anyone?), with the ability to not only practice reading but also writing, while learning not only the shape of the kanji but also the right stroke order. All of that out of the box, without having to find, download, and configure your decks.

    Why is it ideal for KAI School students?

    As of this writing, I've been both developing and using Kanji Sensei for and with the content studied at KAI. You'll find decks developed to suit KAI's studying materials and exams. If you're a KAI student, the app in its current form is specifically well suited for you.

    Will the app be available for Android devices?

    At the time of this writing, only the iOS version of the app has been thoroughly tested, but an Android version is on the way. If you're using an Android device and are interested in testing the app, please drop me a line.

    How can I contribute to Kanji Sensei?

    Right now the app is in pre-beta version, and the best way to contribute is by using it, noting down any bugs or annoyances, and suggest any features or improvements. If you want to do more, feel free to contact me.
